KOERS allows you to explore new roads with just your Pebble smartwatch. Select rides with minimal effort from your phone and follow the line.

Simple & fast

KOERS distinguishes itself by allowing you to select routes and activities with minimal effort. Whether you are up for a quick ride or want to elaborately plan a new adventure, KOERS helps you to find new roads.

Let yourself be surprised by the most beautiful cycling routes nearby.

Plan your next adventure with your favourite route builder.

Use your Strava ride history and friends. Navigate to and along segments.

Power of your phone & simplicity of your Pebble

Features

  • Course navigation on Pebble: show current location on your route.
  • Show distance and direction to route when not on screen.
  • Map rotation based on heading (default) or device orientation.
  • Offline import of GPX and TCX files. iOS only
  • Search for existing routes nearby you.
  • Connect with Strava to select routes, activities and segments.
  • Show current speed and time.
  • Turn backlight always on.

Proven in practice
